Our musical events are curated to enchant a multitude of audiences. From classical music to movie music to opera and chamber music, we perform classical and modern orchestral compositions for all music lovers. Embrace the sheer joy of live performance, at the most inspiring musical events in Brisbane. Our uplifting Morning Masterworks will delight and excite   you, whilst our moving choral performances are dynamic and heartfelt. All event and booking information is available here online. Secure your seat for a captivating symphonic experience today. Our Maestro Series showcases the most renowned classical music, such as Tchaikovsky, Beethoven, Mozart and more, and our Special Events deliver the most talked about blockbuster performances of the musical season. Our breathtaking performances are also available state wide. Explore our regional touring schedule to find an upcoming symphony concert near you. Queensland Symphony Orchestra brings together a stellar line-up of conductors including our Music Director Alondra de la Parra, prestigious soloists and celebrated orchestral talent. Our acclaimed concert season will move and transport you.
